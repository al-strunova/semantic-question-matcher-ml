About This Search Engine

This search engine uses AI-powered semantic matching to find similar questions in the Quora Question Pairs (QQP) dataset. Unlike traditional keyword search, our system understands the meaning behind your questions.

The QQP dataset contains question pairs from Quora covering a wide variety of topics including life advice, politics, technology, and general knowledge. Our semantic search helps identify questions with similar meaning regardless of the specific words used.
